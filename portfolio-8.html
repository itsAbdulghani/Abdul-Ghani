Introducing ClinicControl, a comprehensive online clinic platform designed to streamline medical appointments and management. Developed using PHP, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, and jQuery, ClinicControl facilitates a seamless experience for patients, doctors, and administrators. Patients can easily book appointments, doctors can review and prescribe treatment, and admins maintain complete oversight of the system. This platform showcases my commitment to integrating technology with healthcare to enhance efficiency and user experience...
